Nathaniel Tucker

About

I am currently a PhD candidate in the Center for Control, Dynamical Systems, and Computation (CCDC) at UCSB. I research in the Smart Infrastructure Systems Lab (SISL) in the Department of Electrical and Computer Engineering. My research advisor is Mahnoosh Alizadeh.

I received my Bachelor's degree from Santa Clara University in June 2016 with Electrical Engineering and Computer Engineering majors. I received my Master's degree (with thesis) from Santa Clara University in June 2017 studying Electrical Engineering with emphasis in Communication and Systems. I started my PhD at UCSB in September 2017.

Research Interests

  • Optimization of Smart Grid Technologies
  • Renewable Energy Integration
  • Electric Transportation Systems
  • Urban Planning / Smart Cities / Architecture
